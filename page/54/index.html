Baysian

$$
P(A|C) = \frac{P(A\cap C)}{P(C)},
P(C|A) = \frac{P(A\cap C)}{P(A)} \
Thus, P(A|C) = \frac{P(C|A) * P(A)}{P(C)}
$$

Independent

$$
P(A|C) = \frac{P(A\cap C)}{P(C)}=\frac{P(A)P(C)}{P(C)}=P(A) \
P(C|A) = \frac{P(A\cap C)}{P(A)}=\frac{P(A)P(C)}{P(A)}=P(C) \
%Thus, P(A|C) = \frac{P(C|A) * P(A)}{P(C)}
