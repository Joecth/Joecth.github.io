General form
$$
\begin{equation*}
\begin{array}{rcl}
y :=
\left( \begin{array}{c | c | c | c}
a_0  a_1  \cdots  a_{n-1} 
\end{array} \right)
\left( \begin{array}{c}
\chi_0 \\ \hline
\chi_1 \\ \hline
\vdots \\ \hline
\chi_{n-1}
\end{array}
\right) + y \\
=
\chi_0 a_0 + \chi_1 a_1 + \cdots + \chi_{n-1} a_{n-1} + y.
\end{array}
\end{equation*}
$$
